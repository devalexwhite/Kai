# Kai

The place for hosting community gatherings.

---

![Dashboard of Kai](/screenshots/dashboard.png)

Kai is centered around community. Create a group, post events and be discovered. Find your next book club, C64 enthusiast gathering, poetry reading and more.

Open Source, no algorithms or limits. Community belongs to you, not a corporation.
